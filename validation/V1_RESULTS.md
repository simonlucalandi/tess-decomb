## Flip-type analysis (the part that matters)
Of the 37 K-sensitive objects, **33 are benign survived<->inconclusive threshold crossings**
(inconclusive defers to other evidence; it never kills). Only 4 involve a decision class:
- **3477 (catalog CONFIRMED, independently ZTF-blind-confirmed at FAP 7.8e-19): killed only at K>=6.**
  A ground-truth-protected real signal is destroyed by rank 6+ -> direct evidence that K>=6
  OVER-SUBTRACTS. Upper bracket.
- **4942 (known artifact, never adopted): survives at K=3, killed at K>=4.** Rank 3 is too weak to
  capture the systematic -> lower bracket.
- 3127 (catalog KILL rested on comb-tooth + empty prewhitening + verify, not decomb alone) and 3372
  (non-catalog): gray-zone shuffles, no catalog impact.

**Conclusion: K is bracketed by ground truth to [4,5]; K=5 is the conservative top of the bracket.**
Phase A shows the holdout plateau spans [2,8] (most gain by K~2, no overfit through 8), so within
the plateau the bracket [4,5] from real kill/save ground truth is the binding constraint. The
previously "ad hoc" K=5 is now a derived operating point. RNAAS statement updated accordingly.
